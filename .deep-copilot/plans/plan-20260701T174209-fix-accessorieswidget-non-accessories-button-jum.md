## 目标 / Goal
Fix the "не аксессуары" button in the AccessoriesWidget — it currently causes the tile to "jump" and the button does not stay pressed due to AnimatePresence re-animating when detail content changes.

## 方案 / Approach
Add a stable `key` prop to the `motion.div` inside `TileWrapper`'s `AnimatePresence`. Currently, when the `detail` prop changes (e.g., when scope toggles between accessories/non-accessories), React may treat the old and new `detail` elements as different children, causing `AnimatePresence` to fire exit+enter animations — the detail collapses to height:0 then re-expands, making the tile "jump" and the button appear to un-press. A stable key makes `AnimatePresence` recognize they are the same logical panel.
